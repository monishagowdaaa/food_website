Mango Juice

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ripe mangoes (or enough for 2 cups of chopped mango)
  • ¼ cup water or any fruit juice (optional)
  • 2-3 tablespoons sugar or honey (optional, adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • 2 cups chopped apple

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Wash and dry the mangoes. You can chill them in the fridge for an extra refreshing drink.
  2. Peel the mangoes and chop them into cubes.
  3. Pour water or fruit juice or coconut water.
  4. If you prefer a sweeter juice, add sugar or honey to taste.
  5. Add ice cubes for a chilled drink .
  6. Blend everything until smooth.
  7. Taste and adjust the sweetness or consistency to your preference.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your fresh mango juice!

Mixed Berry Smoothie

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 - 1 ½ cups liquid (milk, yogurt, plant-based milk, coconut water, juice)
  • ½ banana and 1 - 1 ½ cups mixed berries
  • Honey, maple syrup, or other sweetener (to taste)
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Chia seeds, flaxseeds, or protein powder (for extra texture and nutrients)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. If using fresh berries, wash and pat them dry.
  2. Add all your ingredients to a blender.
  3. Begin blending on low speed and gradually increase to high.
  4. If your smoothie is too thick, add more liquid a little at a time until you reach your desired consistency.
  5. Once smooth, taste your smoothie and adjust sweetness or add more berries for flavor.
  6. Pour into glasses and enjoy immediately!

Potato Crisp

INGREDIENTS

  • 2-3 Russet potatoes
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Optional seasonings (e.g., paprika, garlic powder)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Wash and dry the potatoes. Using a mandoline slicer or a sharp knife, thinly slice the potatoes.
  2. In a large pot or deep fryer, heat oil to 350°F (175°C).
  3. carefully add the potato slices to the hot oil. Don't overcrowd the pot, as this can lower the oil temperature and make soggy chips.
  4. Fry the chips for 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Immediately sprinkle the chips with salt and your desired seasonings while they're hot.
  6. Pour into glasses and enjoy immediately!

Chicken Biriyani

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 kg chicken pieces
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder and 1 teaspoon biryani masala
  • Salt and Oil for cooking
  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • Chopped coriander leaves

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mix ginger-garlic paste, spices, and salt in a bowl. Add chicken, coat well, and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Rinse the rice and cook it with salt and water.
  3. Heat oil in a pot. Add half the rice, then half the chicken. Sprinkle with coriander leaves. Repeat with remaining rice and chicken. Cover tightly.
  4. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Let the biryani rest for 10 minutes, Garnish with coriander leaves.

Choco Rush Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips (or chopped dark chocolate)
  • Chocolate shavings
  • Chocolate sprinkles
  • Chocolate candies

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat your oven and bake your chosen chocolate cake recipe in a pan according to instructions. Let the cake cool completely.
  2. Heat the heavy cream in a saucepan over medium heat until simmering. Remove from heat and add the chocolate chips. Let sit for 5 minutes, then whisk until smooth and glossy.
  3. Once the cake is cool, place it on a serving platter. Pour the ganache over the cake, letting it drip down the sides.
  4. While the ganache is still wet, add your desired chocolate decorations like chocolate shavings, sprinkles, or chopped candies. Let the ganache harden completely before serving.
